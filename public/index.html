The Method

What is Heguru?

Founded in Japan by Ruiko and Hirotada Henmi, the Heguru Method is a right-brain development programme designed for children from infancy through primary school. It activates the immense potential of the right hemisphere through carefully structured, high-speed sensory lessons.

Unlike conventional teaching that emphasises repetition and logic, Heguru engages the intuitive, creative brain — the part responsible for photographic memory, speed reading, and extraordinary processing ability. The result is a child who learns faster, thinks deeper, and creates freely.

01

Right-Brain Activation

High-speed, image-rich lessons stimulate the right hemisphere, unlocking photographic memory and rapid information processing.

02

Whole-Brain Integration

As children grow, left and right hemispheres learn to work together — producing balanced thinkers with both creativity and analytical skill.

03

Joyful Learning

Lessons are fast-paced, varied, and playful. Children are never pressured — engagement comes naturally through curiosity and wonder.
